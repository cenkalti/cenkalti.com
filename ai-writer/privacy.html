AI Writer Privacy Policy

Introduction:

AI Writer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data and privacy to reassure our users about the security of their information.

No Data Collection:

AI Writer does not collect or store any personal data from its users. All interactions with the app are processed locally on the user's device.

Interactions with OpenAI API:

  • The app sends text selections directly to the OpenAI API for processing based on the user's instructions.
  • Users must provide their own OpenAI API key, which the app uses to communicate with OpenAI services. This key is not collected, stored, or shared by AI Writer.

User Responsibilities:

  • Users are responsible for the security of their OpenAI API key and for any data they choose to process through the app.
  • It is the user's responsibility to adhere to OpenAI's terms of service and privacy policy when using their API.

No Sharing of Information:

Since AI Writer does not collect data, there is no sharing or disclosure of personal information to third parties.

Data Security:

  • As the app operates entirely offline except for its communication with the OpenAI API, data security is largely dependent on the user's device security measures.
  • Users are encouraged to keep their devices and access credentials secure.

Policy Updates:

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes to our operations or changes in the law. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information:

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact cenkalti at gmail.com.
